Bird Watching


This site is great for all bird watchers. Whether you are a long term bird watcher or are just starting out. This site gives great tips on what to do and the best ways to take pictures, where the best spots are, and so much more!



Common Birds


This site is great if you want to find out what some of the most common bird types are. It gives eight of the most common birds. This site gives a brief explanation of the bird along with pictures of each of them.



Identifying Birds


This site is a great help for identifying birds. If you can't tell what type of bird you are looking at, you can draw a quick, little, detailed sketch to send into them and they will tell you exactly what type of bird it is. They also have posters that you can download for more identification help.



Outdoor Bird Supplies


If you are looking for outdoor supplies to draw those birds to you, this is the perfect place to go! They have a big selection of various supplies for your bird friends.


Donate to Birds

" The rehabilitation of a single injured bird of prey costs up to $1000. Over 300 patients are treated each year at the Wildlife Hospital. 100% of your one time tax deductible donation to the Wildlife Hospital goes towards the care and treatment of the wild birds admitted to World Bird Sanctuary."
